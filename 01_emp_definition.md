# Definition 1 — Endogenous Mirroring Phenomena (EMP)

## Abstract

Endogenous Mirroring Phenomena (EMP) is proposed as a theoretical framework describing the possibility that biological systems exhibit coordinated internal responses that may be reflected across multiple measurable biophysical signals.

At the current stage, EMP should be regarded as a conceptual hypothesis intended to stimulate interdisciplinary research.

---

## Purpose

The objective of the EMP framework is not to replace existing biological theories but to explore whether hidden relationships between biological signals can be described using a unified mathematical language.

---

## Scope

Possible observable domains include

- Optical signals
- Electrical activity
- Spectroscopic responses
- Structural dynamics
- Biochemical perturbations

---

## Current Status

Conceptual hypothesis.

Experimental validation has not yet been established.
