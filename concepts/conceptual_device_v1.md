# Conceptual Diagnostic Device Version 1

## Objective

To explore whether multiple biophysical measurements can be integrated into a conceptual diagnostic framework inspired by EMP.

This document does not describe an existing device.

Instead, it proposes a direction for future engineering discussions.

Possible sensing modalities include

- Raman spectroscopy

- Fluorescence

- UV-Visible spectroscopy

- Electrical impedance

- Infrared spectroscopy

Future revisions may introduce block diagrams and conceptual architectures.
