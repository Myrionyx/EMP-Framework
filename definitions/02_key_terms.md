# Key Terminology

## Biological State

A measurable condition of a biological system at a specific moment.

---

## Mirror State

A hypothetical internal biological state that reflects coordinated changes across multiple biological observables.

---

## Perturbation

Any external or internal factor capable of modifying a biological state.

---

## Mirror Response

The theoretical response produced by a biological system following perturbation.

---

## Observable

Any measurable biological quantity including optical, electrical, biochemical or structural variables.
